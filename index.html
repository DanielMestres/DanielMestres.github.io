A Debate on AI

An essay written for the class ICOM5015 - Artificial Intelligence. Together with two other classmates, we attempt to provide a definitive and well-backed answer to the following three questions: Is Artificial Intelligence really intelligence that is artificial?, Does AI, a man-made intelligence, really play the role of natural intelligence?, Will there ever be an artificial general intelligence comparable or more powerful than human intelligence?

Is Python Fast or Slow?

An assignment for the class ICOM5015 - Artificial Intelligence. A python program for the product of two matrices is developed, both with a standard iterative approach and utilizing the included functions in the Numpy library. With this, we intend to answer the question of whether Python is comparatively slow or just a case of developers not utilizing the appropriate tools for the job.
